My career began at the women's football club Tiverija in Strumica, continued at Skopje 2014 in Skopje, and now I am part of the women's football club Istatov in Skopje.

Since the age of twelve, I have been playing for the women's national football team of Macedonia. I have competed in the U15, U17, and U19 teams, and I am now a member of the senior national team.
